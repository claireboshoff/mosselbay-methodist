Plan Your Visit

We look forward to welcoming you. Here's everything you need to know.

When

Sunday mornings at 09:00. The service lasts about an hour. Arrive a few minutes early to find a seat and settle in.

Where

Queen Street, Mossel Bay. Parking is available on the street. The main entrance faces Queen Street.

What to Wear

Smart-casual is fine. Come as you are — there is no dress code. We want you to feel comfortable.

Children

Children are welcome in the main service. Junior Church (for younger children) runs during the service — they join us for the opening worship.

After the Service

Stay for tea and fellowship. It's a relaxed time to meet people, ask questions, and feel at home.

Questions?

Don't hesitate to reach out before your visit. Call 044 691 2376 or WhatsApp us.
