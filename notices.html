What's Happening

Church Notices

Stay informed about services, events, and opportunities to get involved.

Weekly

Sunday Worship Service

Join us every Sunday at 09:00 for our morning worship service. Everyone is welcome. Children may join Junior Church during the service.

Monthly — 2nd Tuesday

Women's Ministry Meeting

Our Women's Ministry meets on the second Tuesday of each month at 14:30 at the church. A time of fellowship, prayer, and encouragement.

Ongoing

GriefShare Support Group

Our GriefShare programme is available for anyone walking through loss. Contact Rev. Lynne for session times and to register. Open to non-members.

Available

Pastoral Counselling

Confidential counselling is available for members and their families. Contact the church office to arrange an appointment.

Office Hours

Church Office

The church office is open Tuesday to Thursday, 09:00 - 13:00. For urgent pastoral matters outside office hours, please contact Rev. Lynne on WhatsApp: 066 075 5010.
