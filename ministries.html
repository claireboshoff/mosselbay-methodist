Support Ministry

GriefShare

Losing someone you love is one of the most painful experiences in life. GriefShare is a caring community of people who understand what you are going through.

Our group offers a safe space to share your story, learn about the grieving process, and find comfort alongside others who truly understand. You don't have to walk this road alone.

What GriefShare offers:

  • Video-based teaching from grief recovery experts
  • Small group discussion in a confidential setting
  • Personal workbook for reflection between sessions
  • Connection with people who understand your pain

Enquire About GriefShare

"Grief is the price we pay for love. GriefShare helps us carry that price together."

Open to everyone — you do not need to be a church member to attend. Contact us for session dates and times.

Pastoral Care

Counselling Service

Life brings seasons that are difficult to navigate alone. Our church offers confidential pastoral counselling for members and their families.

Whether you're dealing with grief, relationship challenges, anxiety, life transitions, or simply need someone to talk to — our minister and trained counsellors are here to walk alongside you.

Completely confidential.

All conversations are held in strict confidence. There is no judgement here — only care.

Call: 044 691 2376 WhatsApp Rev. Lynne

When to Reach Out

  • Loss of a loved one
  • Marriage or relationship difficulties
  • Anxiety or depression
  • Life transitions and uncertainty
  • Questions about faith
Children's Ministry

Junior Church — Since 1903

Over 120 years of nurturing young hearts in faith. Our Junior Church provides a safe, fun, and engaging space for children to learn about God's love.

Bible Stories

Age-appropriate teaching that brings Scripture to life through stories, activities, and creative learning.

Fun & Friendship

Songs, games, crafts, and activities that help children build friendships and discover faith in an enjoyable way.

Safe Environment

Supervised by caring, experienced leaders who create a nurturing space where every child feels valued and safe.

Junior Church runs during our Sunday 09:00 service. Children are welcome to join the main congregation for the opening worship before heading to their own programme.
