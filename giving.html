Give

Your generosity supports our ministry, our community, and the work of God in Mossel Bay.

Every Gift Makes a Difference

Your tithes and offerings sustain our worship services, support our ministries like GriefShare and Junior Church, maintain our building, and enable us to serve the wider Mossel Bay community. Thank you for your faithfulness.
