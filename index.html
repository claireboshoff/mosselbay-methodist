A Community That Cares

For over a century, Mossel Bay Methodist Church has been a cornerstone of faith and community in our town. We are part of the Methodist Church of Southern Africa, rooted in the Wesleyan tradition of personal holiness, social justice, and community transformation.

Whether you are looking for a church home, seeking support through a difficult season, or simply curious about faith — you are welcome here. Our doors and our hearts are open.

What to Expect on Sunday

Whether it's your first time at church or you're finding a new spiritual home, here's what a Sunday morning looks like.

1

Arrive & Be Welcomed

Our service starts at 09:00. Arrive a few minutes early and you'll be warmly greeted. Dress is smart-casual — come as you are.

2

Worship Together

Enjoy a blend of traditional hymns and contemporary worship. Rev. Lynne Walters leads our service with a relevant, encouraging message.

3

Connect Over Tea

After the service, join us for tea and fellowship. It's the perfect time to meet people and ask questions. Children are welcome throughout.
